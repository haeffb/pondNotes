pondNotes Help

pondNotes provides a Simplenote client for HP webOS. Take notes on your webOS device and access them anywhere. pondNotes will sync with Simplenote so that you can access your notes from any web browser, or from clients on most other mobile platforms.

Creating Notes

Enter your first note by tapping on the '+' icon next to the search bar.

You can also enter a note by typing in the search bar and tapping Enter on the keypad.

Notes are saved as you type. If you make a mistake, and need to revert to a previous version, use the Simplenote web application, which allows you to choose from all versions a note.

Work with Simplenote

Tap the 'Login Here' button to login to your Simplenote account. This will allow you to sync your notes to SImplenote, creating a backup that you can access from any web browser, or from other Simplenote clients. Tap the "sync" button to sync to Simplenote. Use "Show Sync Log" to see the results of your last sync.

Search your notes

Use the search bar to search through your notes - only notes with text or tags matching the search bar will be displayed.

Filter your notes list by tags using the drop-down list at the top of the notes list.

Tag your notes

Add tags to your notes by typing in the tags field. Or, you can select from your existing tags by tapping on the tag icon on the right side of the tags field.

Sort your notes

Sort your notes by modified date, created date, or alphabetically. Choose your preferred sort options from the Preferences scene.

Add a date or time stamp

Add a date/time stamp to the bottom of your note by tapping the 'clock' icon. By default, date and time are both added. Tap and hold the 'clock' icon to chose date only, time only, or both.

Email your notes

Email the note contents by tapping the 'envelope' icon.

Delete notes

Delete a note by tapping the 'trash' icon or by swiping the note from the notes list.
